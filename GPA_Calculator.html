GPA Information

Grade Point Average (GPA) is a standardized way of measuring academic achievement in the U.S. and other countries. It's calculated by taking the average of grade points earned in all courses.

How to Calculate GPA

  • For each course, multiply the grade points by the credit hours
  • Sum all the grade points × credit hours results
  • Divide by the total number of credit hours
  • The result is your GPA on the selected scale
  • Example: (3 credits × 4.0) + (3 credits × 3.7) = 23.1 ÷ 6 credits = 3.85 GPA
