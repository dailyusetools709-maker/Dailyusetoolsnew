About Salary Calculation

This calculator helps you understand how your gross salary translates to take-home pay after taxes and deductions. The calculation includes federal tax (estimated), state tax, FICA (Social Security and Medicare), and common deductions.

Federal Tax

Federal income tax is calculated using progressive tax brackets. The calculator estimates based on your filing status and income level.

State Tax

State income tax rates vary by location. Some states have no income tax, while others have rates up to 13%. Select your state for an estimate.

FICA Taxes

FICA includes Social Security (6.2%) and Medicare (1.45%) taxes. These are flat rates up to certain income limits.

Take-Home Pay

Your net salary is what you actually receive after all taxes and deductions. This is the amount deposited into your bank account.
