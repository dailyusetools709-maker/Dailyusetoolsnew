Statistical Concepts

Mean (Average)

The mean is the sum of all values divided by the number of values. It's the most common measure of central tendency.

Median

The median is the middle value when data is sorted. If there's an even number of values, it's the average of the two middle values.

Standard Deviation

Standard deviation measures how spread out the data is from the mean. A higher value indicates greater variability.
