About Loan Calculator

What is a Loan Calculator?

A loan calculator helps you determine the monthly payment amount for a loan based on the principal amount, interest rate, and loan term. It also calculates the total interest you'll pay over the life of the loan.

How It Works

Our calculator uses the standard loan amortization formula:

M = P [ i(1 + i)^n ] / [ (1 + i)^n – 1 ]

Where:

  • M = Monthly payment
  • P = Principal loan amount
  • i = Monthly interest rate (annual rate ÷ 12)
  • n = Total number of payments (loan term in years × 12)

Factors Affecting Your Loan

  • Credit Score: Higher scores typically qualify for lower interest rates
  • Loan Term: Longer terms reduce monthly payments but increase total interest
  • Down Payment: Larger down payments reduce the principal amount
  • Interest Rate Type: Fixed rates stay the same; variable rates can change
